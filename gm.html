🔍 Search

Where do you want to go?

“Where do you want to go?”
That’s always the first question.
Not where you are.
Not what you’ve already figured out.
Just… where do you want to go?

Some people seem to have clear answers —
college, careers, a life that looks neatly planned.

Some of us type things we don’t fully understand:
“something better”
“somewhere else”
“anything but this”

And sometimes, we don’t search at all.
We just stare at the map…
