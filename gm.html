🔍 Search

Where do you want to go?

“Where do you want to go?” That’s always the first question. Not where you are. Not what you’ve already figured out. Just… where do you want to go? Some people seem to have clear answers — college, careers, a life that looks neatly planned. Some of us type things we don’t fully understand into the search bar: “something better” “somewhere else” “anything but this” And sometimes, we don’t search at all. We just stare at the map… waiting for it to explain us to ourselves.

📍 Current Location

You are here. A small blue dot. Easy to overlook. Easy to underestimate. Not impressive. Not final. Not everything. Just… here. Maybe this isn’t where you thought you’d be. Maybe you expected to feel ahead, clearer, more certain. But maps don’t judge locations. They don’t label places as behind or wrong. They simply show what is. And right now — this is your point on the map.

🔎 Zoom In

Zoom in too much, and everything starts to feel heavier than it is. Mistakes look permanent. Confusion feels like failure. One bad day begins to resemble your entire story. You notice every wrong turn. Every delay. Every place you didn’t reach. And suddenly, it feels like you’re stuck.

🌐 Zoom Out

Then you zoom out. And everything shifts. Your current location becomes small — not insignificant, just not everything. There are roads you haven’t taken. Places you haven’t discovered. Directions you haven’t even considered yet. You’re not stuck. You’re just… seeing too little of the map.

🧭 Nearby Options

Look around. Not at the entire world — just what’s close. A small opportunity. A conversation you’ve been avoiding. A habit you could begin. A step that doesn’t look big… but still moves you forward. Maps don’t show your entire journey at once. They show what’s nearby first. Maybe that’s not limiting. Maybe that’s exactly how it’s meant to be.

🔁 Recalculating…

Sometimes you miss a turn. Sometimes things don’t go the way you planned — or the way you thought they were supposed to. There’s a pause. A quiet moment where everything feels slightly off. And then: Recalculating. No judgment. No “you failed.” No going back to prove anything. Just… a new route. Maybe not the one you imagined. But still leading somewhere.

📌 Saved Places

There are places you return to. Memories. People. Versions of yourself you miss. Some of them are worth revisiting. Some of them only feel good because they’re familiar. Not everything you’ve saved is meant to be your destination. Some things are just… reminders of where you’ve been.

🚫 No Route Found

There will be moments when nothing makes sense. No clear direction. No obvious next step. No route appearing on the screen. And that feeling — it’s uncomfortable. But maybe not everything is meant to be pre-calculated. Some paths don’t show up until you start moving.

🌙 Final View

You don’t need the whole route figured out. You don’t need to be at the “right” place by now. You don’t need perfect timing, perfect clarity, or perfect direction. Because this isn’t about reaching a fixed destination. It’s a map. And maps aren’t meant to tell you where you should be — they help you understand where you are… and remind you that you can still move. 📍 You are here. And that’s not a bad place to be but maybe try going forward.
