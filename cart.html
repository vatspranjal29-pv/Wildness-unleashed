🧾 YOUR CART IS WAITING

You weren’t planning to buy anything.

Just a quick scroll.

Just passing time.

Just seeing what’s there.

But one thing led to another.

A pair of Nike Air Force 1.

Then those Levi’s 501 Jeans you’ve seen everywhere.

The Apple AirPods Pro you imagined using on quiet evenings.

You opened tabs.

Closed them.

Opened them again.

“Not now,” you told yourself.

And still—you added them to your cart.

...

🛒 Items Saved

It’s strange how easy it is to save things.

No pressure.

No commitment.

Just a quiet “maybe.”

Maybe later.

Maybe when it feels right.

Maybe when you’re more sure.

You didn’t say no.

You just didn’t say yes.

...

And then one day, something’s gone.

No warning.

No moment where you noticed it slipping away.

It just… isn’t there anymore.

Not everything waits for you to decide.

Some things leave quietly.

...

Then there are things you once hesitated over.

Too expensive. Too much. Too early.

What once felt out of reach now feels possible.

...

This was never just a cart.

They were decisions you delayed.

Versions of you that felt right—but required you to choose them.

The one who starts before feeling ready

The one who stops overthinking

The one who actually goes for it

You didn’t lose them.

You just kept postponing becoming them.
