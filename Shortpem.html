⚡ Short Poems

🔥 Break the chains, ignite the sky,
🌊 Waves may crash, but I will fly.
🌌 In chaos I find my creation,
⚡ Wildness fuels my liberation.

🌪️ Storms may roar, but I won’t bend,
🔥 My wild journey will never end.
✨ From flaws I carve my own foundation,
🌈 Chaos sparks my transformation.

⚔️ Rebel heart, untamed and free,
🌌 Writing my raw destiny.
🔥 From ashes rises revelation,
⚡ Power born of imperfection.
