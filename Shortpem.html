Echoes in Verse ✨

Alone in a Crowd 🌑

Surrounded by voices, yet none hear mine,
A room full of light, but no warmth to find.
The world moves around, its rhythm so loud,
Yet I stand alone, lost in the crowd.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻

Ignite the Sky 🔥

Break the chains, ignite the sky,
Waves may crash, but I will fly.
In chaos I find my creation,
Wildness fuels my liberation.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻

Storms Within 🌪️

Storms may roar, but I won’t bend,
My wild journey will never end.
From flaws I carve my own foundation,
Chaos sparks my transformation.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻

Rebel’s Flame ⚔️

Rebel heart, untamed and free,
Writing my raw destiny.
From ashes rises revelation,
Power born of imperfection.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻

Quick Strides ⚡💥

Dreams Aren’t Distant ✨

Dreams aren’t distant, they start with you,
Each step forward makes the sky more true.
Rise with courage, let your fire ignite,
Greatness is born when you dare to fight.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻

Fuel the Fire 🔥

Fuel the fire, break the mold,
Chase the future, fierce and bold.
Every setback builds my climb,
I turn the struggle into shine.

— By Pranjal Vats 🌻
