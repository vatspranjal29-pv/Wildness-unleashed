🌑🔥 The Villain I Became 💔⚡

By Pranjal Vats 🌻

There’s a lot I try to be, A thousand shades inside of me. Dreams I chase, but lose the way, Plans half-built that drift away. I wear my hope like broken steel, Fight for things I never heal. I try to stand, to save, to mend, Yet break the hearts I meant to defend. A hero’s crown I tried to claim, But mirrors whisper back my name— Not carved in gold, but scarred and thin, The hero fades, the villain steps in. Still I rise, still I fight, Through every wrong that dims my light. For even villains, torn and true, Are heroes in a different view.🖤🤍 By Pranjal Vats 🌻
